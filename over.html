A BRIEF OVERVIEW OF THE FIVE MISSIONS CARRIED OUT BY ISRO

Mission Date Outcome Key Mission Budget
Aryabhata April 19,1975 Successful First Indian satellite; marked India’s entry into space ₹3 crores (approx.)
PSLV September 20, 1993 Successful First reliable launch vehicle; used for multiple satellite missions ₹170 crores (approx.)
Chandrayaan-1 October 22, 2008 Successful First lunar mission; discovered water molecules on the Moon ₹386 crores
Chandrayaan-2 July 22, 2019 Partially Successful Second lunar mission; orbiter successful, lander lost communication during descent ₹960 crores
Chandrayaan-3 July 14, 2023 Successful Third lunar mission; achieved historic landing near lunar south pole ₹615 crores
Mangalyaan November 5, 2013 Successful First Mars mission; India succeeded on its maiden attempt to Mars ₹450 crores
Gaganyaan Expected 2024-2025 Pending India’s first human spaceflight mission; aims to send Indian astronauts ₹10,000 crores (estimated)
